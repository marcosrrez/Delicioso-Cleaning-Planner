The Delicioso Guide

Why this works for ADHD:

The Delicioso system is built on Cognitive Ease. Traditional lists are overwhelming because they don't account for the "Setup Cost" of starting a task. By color-coding zones, we reduce the mental friction of moving between rooms.

üåà The Color System

  • K Kitchen: High energy, high reward.
  • L Living: Visual peace for relaxation.
  • B Bathroom: Essential hygiene maintenance.
  • R Rest: Protecting your sleep sanctuary.
  • D Deep: Low frequency, high impact.

‚è± The 5-Minute Rule

If you're feeling stuck, pick one task that takes less than 5 minutes. Set a timer. When it goes off, you have permission to stop. Usually, the momentum will carry you forward.
